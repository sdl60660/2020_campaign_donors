Finally, we can look at donors who contributed to multiple candidates to better understand overlap between candidates. The diagram below shows candidates who share individual donors with the selected candidate in the center. The higher the overlap, the larger the outside candidate's "bubble" is.


You can hover over any bubble for more details and click on any bubble to switch the center candidate.


Note that by default, the diagram will display "outbound" overlap (designated with blue arrows), meaning it shows the percentage of the selected candidate's donors who donated to the outside candidate. It can be toggled to instead display "inbound" overlap (designated with the green arrows), meaning the percentage of the outside candidate's donors who donated to the selected candidate.
